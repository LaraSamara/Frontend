Throne and Liberty is a free-to-play multi-platform MMORPG from NCSoft and Amazon Games. The open-world game features large-scale PvPvE in which players can explore everything from sprawling plains to mountain peaks. To survive, players will need to be able to adapt and make decisions based on weather, time of day, and – of course – what other players are doing. The ultimate goal is to claim the throne by defeating Kazar. To do this, players will need to make use of the ability to transform and take the battle to not just the land, but also the sea and air. The game features cross-play bringing together players on PC, Xbox Series X}S, and PlayStation 5. Players on the PS5 can choose to disable cross-play. It’s also worth noting that cross-progression is not supported.
